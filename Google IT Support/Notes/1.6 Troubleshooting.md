## üîç Troubleshooting Methods

- **Isolate the Problem** - Ask questions to narrow the scope and more accurately pinpoint the **root cause**.
  - **Root Cause** - The main factor that's causing a range of issues.

- **Follow the Cookie Crumbs** - This method requires you to go back to when the problem first started, and work forward from there.
  - You can gather information from both users and logs.  
  - Logs act like your system‚Äôs diary, keeping information about dates and events that occurred. You can dig through logs at the exact time a failure happened and potentially find defining events that caused your issue.

- **Start with the Quickest Step First**
